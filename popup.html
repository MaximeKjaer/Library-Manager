  1. Welcome!

    And thank you for installing Library Manager! This is your first visit, and it is possible that everything hasn't quite been loaded yet.
    But no worries! Let us take a tour of the extension meanwhile.

  2. File

    Offline and easy access to your favorite libraries


    The latest version of the libraries you have selected will automatically be stored on what is called the FileSystem, a hidden, sandboxed and protected directory. Clicking on 'File' will allow you to download this libary to your Downloads folder, even when you are offline!
    In other words, no need to go to the library's website to grab the latest version, it will be available here whenever you need it.
